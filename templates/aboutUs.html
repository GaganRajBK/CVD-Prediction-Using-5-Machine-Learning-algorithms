About Us

Welcome to the CardioVascular Disease Predictor! This platform uses machine learning models to predict the risk of heart disease based on various health parameters.

Our goal is to provide individuals with insights into their heart health and help them make informed decisions about their well-being. We use advanced algorithms and data analysis to assess the likelihood of heart disease based on features like age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, exercise habits, and more.

Our Team

We are a team of data scientists, healthcare professionals, and software developers who are passionate about using technology to improve healthcare outcomes. Our team works tirelessly to bring you reliable predictions and useful health information to help you live a healthier life.

How It Works

  • Users input health data such as age, cholesterol level, and exercise habits.
  • The platform uses pre-trained machine learning models to predict the likelihood of heart disease.
  • Results are displayed with a percentage chance of having heart disease.
  • We provide recommendations based on your risk level and encourage seeking medical advice for further evaluation.
